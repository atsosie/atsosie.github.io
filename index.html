Developer + Designer

I'm an analytical thinker with an eye for detail and precision. I love learning new things and discovering creative ways to communicate or tell a story.

After working as a graphic designer and copyeditor for ten years, I learned that my creativity, analytical mind, keen eye for detail and precision, and ability to learn quickly and solve puzzles would make me an excellent developer. My experience with Lighthouse Labs in Vancouver introduced me to an inspiring community of developers and unlocked new opportunities.

Now I am following my passion for storytelling and visual media by designing and crafting software that delivers real value to the user along with a satisfying, accessible user experience.
