About Me

My name is Amber Tsosie (pronounced "Soh-see")

  • What I do ...

    I'm a junior full-stack developer with a background in design and illustration. I bring a range of art and design disciplines to every project, and I enjoy melding those disciplines with my expanding web development skill set.

  • Professionally ...

    During the last two years, I've been working remotely on freelance projects. I'm comfortable working alone and wearing many hats, but I prefer having a team and collaborating with other creative minds.

  • Personally ...

    I'm an analytical thinker and an introvert, but that doesn't stop me from enjoying stimulating conversation or a good dance party. I love learning new things and finding creative ways to communicate or tell a story. I also love sculpting order from chaos with precision and a bit of asymmetry. Details matter.

  • My background ...

    I grew up in lovely Central Texas, spent a little bit of time over the years travelling around North America and Europe, and now I'm based in Vancouver, Canada. As a kid, my goal in life was to become a Renaissance woman, learning various trades and skills. I'm still pursuing that goal.
