Using the company's existing brand elements, I designed and produced materials for print and digital marketing. Deliverables included brochures, ads, event flyers, photography, t-shirt designs, website content, etc.

I started working at Royalty Pecan Farms in 2009 and transitioned to remote contract work in 2015. Over the years I’ve served several roles for the company, including retail manager, orchard tour guide, webmaster, bookkeeper, and even bartender at special events.

I’ve enjoyed my time with the Royalty Pecans family, and I highly recommend you visit the farm or at least try some of the “Pecan Delicacies” if you have the chance (especially if you’ve never had fresh pecans - you don’t know what you’re missing).
